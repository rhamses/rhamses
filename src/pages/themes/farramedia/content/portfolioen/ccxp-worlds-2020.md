Amid the social isolation of 2020, the largest in-person geek culture event in the world needed to reinvent itself in the virtual world.


CCXP Worlds was an ambitious project by the Omelete Company in which all the face-to-face panels of the event were transformed into a virtual experience. In all, 150 hours of content were broadcast, on 5 simultaneous stages over 3 days of event, which reached the public through an interactive gamified map. Numerous national and international panels reflected the major releases of cinema and television in 2020 and from major studios such as Paramount, Warner Media, Amazon Studios, Disney, etc. A great creation and production challenge developed in 2 months.
